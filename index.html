The "AI as the Gateway Drug" Strategy

The Core Play

Use AI consulting as your **wedge** into comprehensive transformation services. Districts desperately need AI help NOW. This urgent need gets you in the door for small projects that naturally expand to comprehensive engagements.

Three-Step Execution

1. Land

Launch "AI Essentials" at accessible price points. Build credibility through quick wins and visible success stories.

2. Expand

Convert initial AI clients to broader services (strategic planning, leadership development). Launch subscription platforms.

3. Lead

Position as Michigan's premier education transformation partner. Majority of revenue from comprehensive services.

Marketing Campaign: "AI Isn't Scary. Being Left Behind Is."

Phase 1: DISRUPT

"The AI Wake-Up Call"

Content Shock Strategy, Fear-to-Action Tactics

Phase 2: DEMONSTRATE

"See It In Action"

Success Story Blitz, Try-Before-You-Buy

Phase 3: DOMINATE

"The Movement Has Started"

FOMO Creation, Community Building

Key Campaign Assets

  • Hero Asset: "The Michigan Educator's Guide to AI" (50-page definitive resource)
  • Assessment Tool: "Is Your District AI-Ready?" (interactive quiz)
  • Calculator: "AI ROI Calculator for Schools" (shows time/money saved)
  • Comparison Chart: "AI Tools for Education: What's Safe, What's Not"

The Psychology Play & Bottom Line

Sell Confidence, Not Consulting

  • Build Trust Through Transparency
  • Create Urgency Without Panic
  • Make It About Them: their students, their teachers, their future

AI is just the conversation starter for your real value proposition: making Michigan Virtual the essential partner for educational transformation.
